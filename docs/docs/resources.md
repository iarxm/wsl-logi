# List Of Excellent Physical Training Resources

- Andy Galpin
    - Gold standard high performance testing methodologies
- Christopher Sommer
    - Strength and gymnastic skill specialist. Sommer's work is a highly systematized GST in a very accessible and pragmatic form.
    - [Sommer on Tim Ferris](https://youtu.be/EzyAPEWOlxw?si=tlF0qaIIztdIrizQ)
- Pavel Tsatsouline
    - Strength specialist. Founder of kettle bell training, and a primary source of pioneering ideas on strength.
    - [Pavel on Huberman](https://youtu.be/Z3OpxT65fKw?si=D0XXeZSruMgIQPN9)
    - [Strong First](https://www.strongfirst.com/)
    - [Dragon Door](https://dragondoor.com/)
- Poloquin
- Gymnastics Physical Education Curriculum
    - [Gymnastics PE](https://www.pdst.ie/Gymnastics)
    - [Gymnastics PE](https://www.scoilnet.ie/pdst/primary/pe/strands/Gymnastics/)
- Tom Kurz
    - Expert on flexibility. Root of modern methods of flexibility training. Also an expert on strength.
    - [Tom Kurz Site](https://tomkurz.com/about)
    - [Tom Kurz Strength](https://tomkurz.com/tag/strength-training/)
- Kit Laughlin
    - Gold standard flexibility training
- Emmet
    - Gold standard flexibility training
    - [Emmet head to toes](https://youtu.be/Ydaz_E4xZM0?si=LTS6NBXlgC1XOcYx)
    - [Modern Mobility](https://modernmobility.com/)
- Ido Portal
    - Ido is a primary source of ideas on movement training. His content is less accessible digitally. Highly valuable adhoc principles, and a means of breaking the rigidity of formal systems, into strength as unique practise and expression.
    - [Ido Deep Squat](https://youtu.be/lbozu0DPcYI?si=GXT5ctQnseSdRZAP)
    - [Ido Dynamic Squat](https://youtu.be/xPwG2hqnOx0?si=thrGQF3qGRDLPu4w)
    - [Ido Beginner Crawls](https://youtu.be/ufAUAYrxfIY?si=ExBQ68lo_QhxJ9Ob)
- Jozef and Linda
    - Fighting Monkey
- Dynamic athletic intelligence training
    - [A](https://youtu.be/_6n12rSpMjg?si=1lI9Tkw1AOIy9QDJ)
    - [A](https://youtu.be/av4qO5Rbs_c?si=YmK9V9RqtCeRp4y4)
- Weksler Movement Archery
    - Training as expression
- Quick Demonstrations
    - [Hollow Body Rocks](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SfkuOb_1GK8)
    - [Gymnastic PE Animal Walks](https://vimeo.com/570302275)
